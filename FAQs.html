FAQs
  • I. Start Product Optimization Task
  • 1.1) Minimum account balance of 50 USD for the first 40 tasks/set.
  • 1.2) A minimum renewal of 100 USD is required to start the new tasks.
  • 1.3) Users need to complete all tasks for the day before requesting a withdrawal.

  • II. Withdrawal
  • 2.1) Users need to complete all tasks for the day before requesting a withdrawal.
  • 2.2) You cannot request a withdrawal or refund if you choose to give up or withdraw in the middle of a task optimization.
  • 2.3) No withdrawals can be processed if the user's withdrawal request has not been received.
  • 2.4) The maximum withdrawal amount for VIP1 users is US$10,000, for VIP2 users it is US$20,000, and for VIP3 users it is US$50,000.
  • 2.5) The user account has insufficient reputation score and cannot use all functions of the platform normally.

  • III. Funds
  • 3.1) All funds will be held securely in the user's account and can be requested in full once all data has been completed.
  • 3.2) To avoid any loss of funds, all data will be processed by the system and not manually.
  • 3.3) The platform will take full responsibility for any accidental loss of funds.

  • IV. Account Security
  • 4.1) Please do not disclose your login password and withdrawal password to others.
  • 4.2) Users are not recommended to set their birthday password, ID card number, or mobile phone number as their withdrawal password.
  • 4.3) If you forget your login password or withdrawal password, please contact our online service to reset it.

  • V. General Product Data
  • 5.1) Platform profit is categorized into normal product data profit and 10 times combination product data profit.
  • 5.2) Normal users will earn 0.5% of the profit for each normal task of optimizing.
  • 5.3) VIP2 users will earn 1% of the profit for each normal task of optimizing.

  • VI. Combined Product Data
  • 6.1) Combined Product Data is composed of 0 to 3 data, and the system will randomly allocate the combined data to users.
  • 6.2) Users will receive 10 times the profit for each combined data than for the normal product data.
  • 6.3) After receiving the combined product data, profits will stop rolling once all product data is completed.
  • 6.4) The system will randomly allocate combined data based on the total balance in the user’s account.
  • 6.5) Once allocated, combined product data cannot be canceled or skipped.

  • VII. Deposit
  • 7.1) The deposit amount is the user’s choice, but it’s recommended the user deposits enough to proceed smoothly.
  • 7.2) Deposits should be made according to the insufficient amount shown in the account.
  • 7.3) Deposits must be confirmed by the online service before proceeding.
  • 7.4) The platform is not responsible for wrong deposit details provided by the user.

  • VIII. Merchant Cooperation
  • 8.1) Different merchants on the platform will handle product optimization tasks. If the user does not complete tasks, it will affect merchant progress.
  • 8.2) Merchants will provide deposit details for users to make deposits for specific product optimizations.
  • 8.3) Delays in optimization will affect merchants, and complaints can be raised against users if delays occur.

  • IX. Invitation
  • 9.1) Users can invite others to the platform using their invitation code.
  • 9.2) Users cannot invite others unless they have completed all data tasks.
  • 9.3) Referrers will receive 20% of the referee’s total earnings for the day.

  • X. Operating Hours
  • 10.1) Platform operating hours are from 10:00 am to 22:59 pm (NYT).
  • 10.2) Online service operating hours are from 10:00 am to 22:59 pm (NYT).
  • 10.3) Withdrawal processing times are from 10:00 am to 22:59 pm (NYT).
